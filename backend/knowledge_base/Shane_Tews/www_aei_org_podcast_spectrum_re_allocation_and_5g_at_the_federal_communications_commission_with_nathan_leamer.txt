	Spectrum Reallocation and 5G at the Federal Communications Commission (with Nathan Leamer) | American Enterprise Institute - AEI

What are the challenges of reallocating spectrum for commercial use, and how might the resulting “spectrum wars” hinder the rollout of new networks? Furthermore, how has the current Federal Communications Commission (FCC) approached this issue?

, vice president of public affairs at the strategy firm
Targeted Victory
and former policy advisor to FCC Chairman Ajit Pai, joins
Shane
to discuss spectrum reallocation, 5G, and how the current FCC has succeeded in a number of its policy endeavors.
