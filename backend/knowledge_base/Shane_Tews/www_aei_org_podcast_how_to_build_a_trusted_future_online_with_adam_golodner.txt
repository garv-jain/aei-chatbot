	How to Build a ‘Trusted Future’ Online (with Adam Golodner) | American Enterprise Institute - AEI

How can we
achieve a real, certifiable level of comfort and trust between consumers and
the companies that make our technologies? The standards for what constitutes
trust and safety vary from user to user; we need to find a tractable way to
build out indicators of trust that will allow both producers and users to see
risks clearly.
On this episode of “Explain to Shane,”
Shane
is joined by
Adam Golodner
, co-chair of
Trusted Future
—a new think tank dedicated to enhancing trust in today’s digital ecosystem. Adam and Shane discuss the technical and engineering components of trust and safety, along with what these topics have to do with cybersecurity, privacy, and the supply chain.
