	Analyzing Congress’ Skirmish with Big Tech (with Jennifer Huddleston) | American Enterprise Institute - AEI

With five bills introduced to the House Judiciary Committee in June of this year, Congress is moving swiftly to rein in what it sees as the market power and abusive business practices of “Big Tech” firms — namely Apple, Amazon, Facebook, and Alphabet Inc.’s Google. What is behind Congress’ push for new antitrust laws targeted at these companies, and how would these laws impact consumers?
On this episode,
Shane
and AEI co-host
Mark Jamison
are joined by
NetChoice
Policy Counsel
Jennifer Huddleston
for a conversation on what Congress’ proposals would mean for the future of mergers and acquisitions in the tech industry, along with how these proposals would deprive consumers of services they enjoy thanks to Silicon Valley’s culture of “permissionless innovation.”
