	Unpacking the Complexities of Online Content Moderation (with Julie Owono) | American Enterprise Institute - AEI

The debate around
online content moderation is not slowing down. People remain bitterly divided
over whether social media platforms should take down more content, or not
moderate at all. But this issue is not limited to the US: Across the world,
authoritarian regimes are using online censorship to silence dissenters, and
are retaliating against citizens who share certain content online. How are
content moderation experts working to solve these ever-complex issues?
On this episode,
Shane
is joined by
Julie Owono
to discuss why we need clear principles around online speech — centered on free expression — and how these principles can be applied across different countries. Julie is the executive director of Stanford University’s
Content Policy & Society Lab
, executive director of
Internet Without Borders
, and a member of the
Facebook Oversight Board
.
