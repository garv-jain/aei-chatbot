	John Tamny on the End of Work | American Enterprise Institute - AEI

In a world where the traditional boundaries of work are being redrawn, the interplay between work and personal satisfaction, a sense of purpose, and meaning is rapidly evolving. This week on Hardly Working, Brent sits down with
John Tamny
, editor of RealClearMarkets and president of the
Parkview Institute
. They discuss Tamny’s 2018
book
The End of Work: Why Your Passion Can Become Your Job
, which envisions a world where material abundance frees all of us up to pursue our passions at work.
