	All in the Family? How Two-parent Households Influence Economic Outcomes | American Enterprise Institute - AEI

Children who are born and raised in two-parent families are significantly more likely to avoid poverty and other adverse social outcomes but how that happens is unclear.
Brent talks to Scott Winship, Director of Poverty Studies at AEI and co-author of a new report on family structure in America entitled “
The Demise of the Happy Two-Parent Home
”. They discuss family stability, economic security, and how family structure contributes to the success for children.
