“The Divided Brain” is a groundbreaking new documentary based on the work of British psychiatrist and literary expert Iain McGilchrist, author of “The Master and His Emissary: The Divided Brain and the Making of the Western World” (Yale University Press, 2009). This documentary explores differences in how the left and right brain hemispheres process information, which may be key to understanding many contemporary social challenges, from the breakdown in political discourse to environmental degradation.
Dr. McGilchrist believes that the left brain hemisphere, responsible for the close analysis required for technological innovation, may be “colonizing” the right hemisphere that manages broad perception and social interaction. If he is right, we may be creating technologies and conditions that inhibit the human relationships and social cohesion required for life in democratic societies and market economies.
