	What Does COVID-19 Mean for the Future of American Cities? | American Enterprise Institute - AEI

Nowhere was the economic shutdown caused by the spread of the coronavirus more apparent than in the nation’s the major cities that became the early epicenters of the pandemic. When cities reopen, will the people and businesses who left come back?
This episode of Hardly Working is an interview
Brent Orrell
did with
Bob Zadek
from the
Bob Zadek Show
. They discuss the changes cities underwent during the virus, the history of cities in times of pandemics, and reflections on what happens next in our urban centers.
