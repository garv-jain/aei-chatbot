PAGE_TITLE: 
	Rethinking Prisoner Reentry | RETHINK TANK | American Enterprise Institute - AEI

VIDEO
Every year, hundreds of thousands of Americans are released from prison,
 and despite great efforts to ensure that these men and women stay on 
the other side of the bars, more than half of them are locked back up in
 a matter of months. AEI’s Brent Orrell examines where existing reentry 
practices fail and what new approaches could be taken to prevent 
recidivism.
