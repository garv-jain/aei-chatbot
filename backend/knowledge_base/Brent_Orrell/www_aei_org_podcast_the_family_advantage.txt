	The Family Advantage | American Enterprise Institute - AEI

Family is the seedbed of skill development for young children: relationships within the family influence a child’s development in all areas of their life. Furthermore, as family structure has morphed and shifted over the past few decades, so have the economic outcomes for children.
In this episode,
Brent Orrell
hosts
Alan Hawkins
of Brigham Young University and
W. Bradford Wilcox
of AEI to discuss marriage, family life, and the economics of family formation. Join us as they discuss the impact of family structure on long-term outcomes for kids.
